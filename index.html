Portfolio Snippets

Literacy Action Center Redesign

The Literacy Action Center helps low-literacy adults with their reading, writing, and math skills, but the original design was not accessible to these learners, nor does we feel it inspires confidence with donors or volunteers.

We believed a more visually-focused, accessible, and aesthetically warm design would intersect to increase learner enrollment and success, volunteer engagement, and donor contributions.

Poppy: Tame the Plant Care Jungle

There are a lot of resources and information at plant owner’s disposal, but rarely do they capture all that’s needed to care for their plant.

We built Poppy to not only let users set up their plant care plan and reminders, but to easily find a community of fellow plant owners to help with questions and issues specific to their plant.
